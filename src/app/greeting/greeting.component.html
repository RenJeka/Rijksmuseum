I greet you!

This is a test application to show you my capabilities in Angular 9.

The application takes information from the database of one famous museum - Rijksmuseum (Netherlands). There are many unique art objects from different centuries. This application gives you the opportunity to view the list of museum art objects, more detailed information about it, and adding your favorite art objects in favorite list.

Also in the application, a keyword search is available (try to enter, for example, the word "war" in the search), data sorting is also present.
